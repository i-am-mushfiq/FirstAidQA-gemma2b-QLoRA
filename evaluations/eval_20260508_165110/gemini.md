### Key Findings

* 
**Severe Bleeding Protocol Failure:** Both models consistently failed to recommend a tourniquet for life-threatening arterial bleeding, opting for "pressure" even when the prompt stated pressure was insufficient.


* 
**Choking Protocol Weakness:** Both models failed the specific protocols for infant choking and the drowning-induced unconsciousness (omitting initial rescue breaths).


* 
**Shock Mismanagement:** Both models provided dangerous advice for shock, suggesting a lateral position for a conscious patient.


* 
**Safety & Escalation Omissions:** Both models frequently failed to explicitly state that the user should call emergency services (000/911) in many SC scenarios.
